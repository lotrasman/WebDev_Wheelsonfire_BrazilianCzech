    About

    I’m John O’Reilly, and truth be told, I’ve always trusted engines more than people—they’re simply easier to understand.

    Wheels on Fire was born from that lifelong passion: my belief that a car isn’t just a way to get from one place to another, but something that can light a fire inside you every time you drive.

    Every vehicle here is chosen with care, carrying the same dedication I’ve poured into every engine I’ve touched.

    John O'Reilly's story

    What began in a single-bay workshop on Dublin’s north side grew into a discreet showroom known for sourcing the most coveted supercars and grand tourers. John has spent more than two decades building relationships with collectors, dealers, and restorers to keep a rotating roster of machines that feel brand-new—because they practically are.

    Every delivery is personal. Clients get a full mechanical report, bespoke detailing, and access to John’s trusted network for maintenance, financing, and performance upgrades.

    “Engines make sense to me. If they’re noisy, I listen harder. If they’re tired, I rebuild them. People trust me with their dream cars because I never rush the work.” — John O'Reilly, Founder
