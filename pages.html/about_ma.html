About

I’m John O’Reilly, and truth be told, I’ve always trusted engines more than people—they’re simply easier to understand.

Wheels on Fire was born from that lifelong passion: my belief that a car isn’t just a way to get from one place to another, but something that can light a fire inside you every time you drive.

Every vehicle here is chosen with care, carrying the same dedication I’ve poured into every engine I’ve touched.

“Every car here was chosen with one purpose: to ignite the same fire in you that’s kept me in this business all my life.” — John O’Reilly
